Our Story

Founded in 2025, Pixero was born from a simple observation: finding and booking quality photography and editing services was unnecessarily complicated. Our founders, a team of photographers, developers, and design enthusiasts, set out to create a platform that would simplify this process while empowering creative professionals.


What started as a small community has grown into a global network connecting thousands of photographers and editors with clients who value quality visual content. Our mission remains unchanged: to democratize access to professional visual services and create opportunities for talented creators worldwide.

Our Mission

At Pixero, we connect the creators with the clients. We're committed to building a platform that serves both creators and clients alike.
